Hult Prize Tanta

Hult Prize at Tanta University is a student-led movement that challenges young minds to solve the world's most pressing issues. We are part of the global Hult Prize initiative, the world's largest student competition for social entrepreneurship. We aim to inspire and equip Tanta University students with the skills and resources to develop impactful business ideas that address critical social and environmental challenges. We believe that business can be a force for good, and we empower students to turn their innovative ideas into sustainable ventures that create positive change. Each year, we focus on the annual Hult Prize Challenge, a theme announced by the Hult Prize Foundation that tackles a pressing global issue. We organize workshops, training sessions, and networking events to help students develop their social ventures around the challenge theme.
